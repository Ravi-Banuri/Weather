How to use Weather App?

  1. On opening application list of cities is shown.
  2. Click on a city name to see today's weather in the city.
  3. Click button in City list scree to pick a city from Map.
  4. On click on in map screen is shown
  5. Place pin on any city and click on button.
  6. On clicking button in Map screen selected city is added to Database and navigates back to City list screen.
  7. In City list screen click on Delete icon to remove city from the list.
  8. Units can be changed to from setting of application.
